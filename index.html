Hello, I'm Kerry

Your wedding deserves
more than photos

I'm Kerry Percy, a wedding content creator based in beautiful Glasgow, Scotland. I believe your wedding day is more than just a date – it's the beginning of your greatest adventure together.

My approach combines cinematic storytelling with an editorial eye, creating content that captures not just how your day looked, but how it felt. From the nervous excitement of getting ready to the joyful chaos of the dance floor, I'm there to preserve every precious moment.

Whether you're planning an intimate elopement in the Scottish Highlands or a grand celebration in the city, I'd love to be part of your story.
